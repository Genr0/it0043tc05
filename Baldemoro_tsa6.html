Guts - The Black Swordsman. A former mercenary who wields a massive sword and seeks revenge against Griffith.

Casca - A brave warrior. Former second-in-command of the Band of the Hawk and a strong-willed woman.

Griffith - The leader of the Band of the Hawk. Ambitious and charismatic, he dreams of his own kingdom.

Puck - A playful elf who becomes Guts' companion and provides comic relief and healing magic.

Serpico - A loyal knight who serves Farnese. Skilled with a rapier and highly intelligent.

Farnese - A noblewoman who commands the Holy Iron Chain Knights and later joins Guts' group.

Kentaro Miura

Kentaro Miura was a Japanese manga artist best known for his acclaimed dark fantasy series Berserk. The manga started in 1989 and continued until Miura's death in 2021. The series has been highly influential, praised for its detailed artwork, storytelling, and complex characters.

History of Berserk

Berserk is set in a medieval Europe-inspired dark fantasy world, centering on Guts, a lone mercenary, and Griffith, the leader of a mercenary band called the Band of the Hawk. The manga explores themes of camaraderie, sacrifice, and the struggle against destiny.
